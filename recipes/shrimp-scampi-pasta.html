Shrimp Scampi Pasta

Delicious Shrimp Scampi Pasta

Follow this recipe to make some delicious Shrimp Scampi Pasta!

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • 16 Oz of Linguine
  • Butter
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Dried Red Pepper Flakes
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Add Linguine to Boiling Water for 6 to 8 Minutes
  2. Add Butter, Shallots, Garlic, and Red Pepper Flakes to Hot Pan for 3 to 4 Minutes
  3. Season Shrimp with Salt and Black Pepper. Add to Skillet and Cook until Pink
  4. Remove Shrimp. Pour White Wine and Lemon Juice into Skillet. Bring to a Boil
  5. Add 2 Tablespoons of Butter and 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil. Simmer
  6. Mix Linguine, Shrimp, and Parsley into Skillet. Drizzle with Olive Oil
