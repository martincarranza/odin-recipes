Chorizo Tacos

Delicious Chorizo Tacos

Follow this recipe to make some delicious Chorizo Tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 Chorizo Sausage
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • 4 Corn Tortillas
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chopped Onion
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chopped Fresh Cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine Crumbled Chorizo and Chipotle Peppers
  2. Cook Chorizo Mixture on Medium-High Heat for 5 to 7 Minutes
  3. Heat Tortillas in Same Skillet Used for Chorizo for 1 to 2 minutes per Side
  4. Use 2 Tortillas for Each Taco. Fill with Chorizo, Onion, and Cilantro
