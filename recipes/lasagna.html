Delicious Lasagna

Follow this recipe to make some delicious Lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef and Italian Sausage
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • One Onion
  • Two Clove of Garlic
  • Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Two Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Two Cans of Tomato Paste
  • Two Tablespoons of Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the Ricotta and Egg Mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna: Meat, Noodles, Cheese, Meat, Parmesan
  5. Bake at 375 F for 50 minutes
